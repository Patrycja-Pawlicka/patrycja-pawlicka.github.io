My Coding Journey

I started looking into coding in early 2023 as a fun way to challenge myself and learn something completely new. Since I was always fascinated by game graphics and creative projects, programming felt like the next natural step. I initially dived into C#, but soon shifted my focus to the exciting world of HTML and CSS. As my skills grew, so did my hunger for knowledge, and I eagerly took on JavaScript as my next adventure. I've been hooked ever since, loving every moment of it. I'm now studying Computer Science at university, eager to turn this passion into a career where I can bring ideas to life on the web and beyond.
