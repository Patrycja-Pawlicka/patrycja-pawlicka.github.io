My Favorite Animals

Fun Animal Facts

  • Elk: Elk can bugle! Males make a loud, high-pitched call during mating season that can travel miles.
  • Polar Bear: Despite their white fur, polar bears actually have black skin underneath to absorb more heat from the sun.
  • Monkey: Some monkeys use tools, like rocks, to crack open nuts.
  • Lion: A lion’s roar can be heard from up to 8 kilometers away — king of the savannah vibes.
  • Sheep: Sheep have excellent memories — they can remember up to 50 different faces of other sheep and humans.
  • Elephant: Elephants are known to mourn their dead, often returning to the bones of lost herd members.
  • Cheetah: Cheetahs are the fastest land animals, reaching speeds up to 112 km/h in short bursts.
  • Rabbit: Rabbits' teeth never stop growing — they need to gnaw constantly to keep them trimmed down.
  • Bear: Bears have a great sense of smell — it's believed to be 7 times better than a bloodhound's!
